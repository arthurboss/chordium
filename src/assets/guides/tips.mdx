# Tips for Formatting

- Align chords with the corresponding lyrics for better readability
- Number your verses (e.g., `[Verse 1]`, `[Verse 2]`)
- Indicate repeats with `x2` (e.g., `[Chorus x2]`)
- Keep lines short and concise for better display on smaller screens
